Game Rules

Overview

Mafia Board Games are thrilling social deduction party games where players are secretly divided into two teams: the Mafia and the Villagers. These exciting board games alternate between night and day phases until one team wins.

Mafia Board Games Rules

  • Total Players: 6-15 players
  • Mafia: Evil players who know each other's identity (1-3 players)
  • Villagers: Regular townspeople trying to identify the Mafia
  • Doctor: A special villager who can save one person from elimination AND poison one person during the game (0-1 player)
  • Detective: A special villager who can investigate one player's identity each night (0-1 player)

Game Flow

  1. Setup Phase:
    • Each player receives a secret role card
    • Players must keep their roles secret unless eliminated
  2. Night Phase:
    • All players "sleep" (close their eyes)
    • Mafia members wake up and choose one player to eliminate
    • Doctor wakes up and can choose to save or poison a player
    • Detective wakes up and can investigate one player's identity
  3. Day Phase:
    • All players "wake up"
    • Night actions results are revealed
    • Players discuss and debate who might be the Mafia
    • Players vote to eliminate one suspected Mafia member

Special Abilities

  • Doctor's Powers:
    • Can save one player (including themselves) once per game
    • Can poison one player once per game
    • Must choose whether to save, poison, or skip each night
  • Detective's Power:
    • Can investigate one player each night
    • Learns whether the investigated player is Mafia or not

Winning Conditions

  • Villagers Win: When all Mafia members are eliminated
  • Mafia Wins: When the number of Mafia equals or exceeds the number of Villagers

Tips for Playing Mafia Board Games

  • Pay attention to player behavior during discussions in these strategic board games
  • Special roles (Doctor and Detective) should be careful about revealing their identity
  • Use voting strategically in mafia board games - sometimes not voting might be the best strategy
  • Mafia members should try to blend in with villagers during discussions
  • These board games work best with consistent group play to develop strategies
