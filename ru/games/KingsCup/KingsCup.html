🎮 Как играть Король чаши Drinking Game

Король чаши - это классическая игра для вечеринок perfect for adult groups! Each card has a special drinking rule that creates fun interactions. The goal is to avoid drawing the 4th King, which means drinking the entire Король чаши!

✨ Установка игры

  • 🃏 One virtual deck of 52 cards
  • 👥 3 or more players for maximum fun
  • 🏆 A virtual "Король чаши" in the center
  • 🥤 Each player has their own drink
  • 🎪 A fun group ready for challenges!

🎯 Как играть

  1. 👤 Игрокs take turns clicking "Вытянуть карту"
  2. 🃏 Follow the rule for the card you drew
  3. 👑 If you draw a King: Pour some of your drink into the Король чаши
  4. 🍻 Draw the 4th King? Ты must drink the entire Король чаши!
  5. 🎉 Game ends when the 4th King drinks the cup!

🔥 Important Rules to Remember

  • 🍻 Напарник (8): Choose a drinking buddy. When you drink, they drink too! This lasts until someone else draws an 8.
  • ❓ Вопросы (Q): Ты can only ask questions. Anyone who answers (instead of asking back) drinks. Example: A asks "What time is it?" B answers "9:30" ❌ B drinks!
  • 📜 Создать правило (J): Create any rule everyone must follow (e.g., "No swearing", "Point with elbows only").
  • 👑 Король чаши (K): Pour some drink into center cup. The unlucky person who draws the 4th King drinks it all!
  • 🌊 Водопад (A): Все пьют continuously. Ты can't stop until the person before you stops!

❓ Frequently Asked Вопросы

🍻 What is Король чаши drinking game?

Король чаши - это классическая игра для вечеринок where players draw cards and follow drinking rules. It's perfect for adult parties and social gatherings!

👥 How many players do you need for Король чаши drinking game?

Король чаши drinking game is best with 3-8 players, but you can play with more! The more players, the more fun and drinking interactions you'll have.

🃏 What happens when all Kings are drawn?

When the 4th King is drawn, that player must drink the entire Король чаши (which contains drinks from the previous 3 Kings)! This ends the game - it's the ultimate challenge!

🔄 Can I customize the drinking game rules?

While our Король чаши drinking game rules are set for consistency, when you draw a Jack (Make a Rule), you can create custom drinking rules for your group during that game session!
