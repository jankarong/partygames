Similar to hedbanz rules but with a twist - try to avoid making the actions or saying the words shown on your card. If you accidentally do the action, you'll be eliminated. The last player remaining wins!

üéÆ How to Play

  1. Choose 2-10 players to participate
  2. Players take turns drawing cards
  3. After drawing a card, that player must avoid doing the action shown on their card
  4. If a player does their forbidden action, other players can point it out, and that player is eliminated
  5. Eliminated players can still observe and point out other players' actions
  6. The game continues until only one player remains

üí° Game Tips

  • Remember: Act natural, don't make obvious attempts to avoid your forbidden action
  • Watch other players carefully for their forbidden actions
  • Stay alert - other players might try to trick you into doing your action
  • Even if eliminated, stay engaged and help spot other players' mistakes
