Headbands Online is a free web-based version of the classic party guessing game. Players wear invisible words on their foreheads and must guess what they are by asking yes or no questions to other players. This engaging online party game is perfect for friends, family, and colleagues who want to play together remotely or in person.

Unlike the physical card game, Headbands Online features an unlimited word database with hundreds of categories, ensuring every game is unique and fun. No cards needed—just a smartphone, tablet, or computer and an internet connection to start playing instantly.

Learning how to play Headbands is simple and fun. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Select number of players: Choose between 2-10 players to customize your game
  2. Pick game duration: Select 30 or 60 seconds per turn based on your preference
  3. Hold your phone to forehead: The word displays on the screen, visible only to other players
  4. Ask yes/no questions: Ask strategic questions to narrow down what word you're wearing
  5. Make guesses: Once you think you know, guess the word by clicking the Correct button
  6. Score points: Earn 1 point for every correct guess during your turn
  7. Pass turns: After your time runs out, the next player takes their turn
  8. Determine winner: The player with the most points when all turns are complete wins

Understanding Headbands rules helps you play strategically:

For the guessing player:

  • Ask broad category questions first: "Am I a person?" "Am I an animal?"
  • Use process of elimination to narrow down possibilities
  • Ask about specific features or characteristics
  • Work quickly to maximize correct guesses before time runs out
  • Pay attention to other players' reactions for subtle clues

For players giving clues:

  • Answer honestly with only "yes" or "no"
  • No hints, descriptions, or additional information allowed
  • Keep responses quick to maintain game pace
  • Maintain fair play and accuracy for a balanced game
