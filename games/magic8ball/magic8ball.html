    About the Magic 8 Ball

    The Magic 8 Ball is a fortune-telling toy invented in 1946 by Albert Carter and Abe Bookman. Originally called the "Syco-Seer," it became one of the most popular toys of the 20th century and remains a cultural icon today.

    Fun Facts About the Magic 8 Ball

    • The traditional Magic 8 Ball contains 20 possible answers
    • It was inducted into the National Toy Hall of Fame in 2018
    • Over 100 million Magic 8 Balls have been sold worldwide
    • The original design was inspired by a spirit writing device used by the inventor's mother
