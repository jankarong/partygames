How to Play Never Have I Ever

Welcome to our collection of never have i ever questions spicy and never have i ever questions dirty! Here's how to play:

1. Players take turns clicking "Next" to get a statement.

2. If you HAVE done it, take a drink or put a finger down.

3. If you have NEVER done it, do nothing.

4. The player with the most fingers up at the end wins!

5. Remember: The spicier and dirtier the questions get, the more fun the game becomes!
