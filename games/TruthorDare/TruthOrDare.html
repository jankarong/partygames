Game Rules

  1. Welcome to our online Truth or Dare game! Players take turns clicking either "TRUTH" or "DARE"
  2. If you choose "TRUTH", you must answer the question honestly
  3. If you choose "DARE", you must perform the given challenge
  4. You cannot change your choice after seeing the question/dare
  5. If you refuse to complete a truth or dare, you must face a penalty decided by the group
  6. Keep all truths and dares appropriate and respectful
  7. Our Truth or Dare creator ensures endless fun - have fun and be a good sport!
