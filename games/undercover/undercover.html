How to Play Undercover

  1. Each player receives a secret word - most players get the same "civilian" word, while undercover players get a similar but different word.
  2. Players take turns describing their word without saying it directly.
  3. After each round, players vote on who they think is the undercover.
  4. The person with the most votes is eliminated.
  5. Game continues until either all undercover players are found (civilians win) or number of undercover equals civilians (undercover wins).

Tips: Be subtle in your descriptions to avoid revealing whether you're a civilian or undercover!
