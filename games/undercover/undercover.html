How to Play Undercover Game Online

Game Features:

  • Undercover & Mr White Modes: Play as Undercover (find the spy) or Mr White (blank card challenge) for varied gameplay
  • 1500+ Word Pairs: Over 1500 word pairs across 15 diverse categories including Food & Drink, Animals, Objects, Jobs & Roles, Entertainment, Nature & Weather, Funny, Sports & Fitness, Technology & Digital, Travel & Places, Relationships, Brands & Companies, Historical Figures, Movies, and Internet Slang
  • Custom Words: Create your own word pairs for personalized Undercover or Mr White gameplay
  • Mobile Optimized: Large, touch-friendly selection buttons for easy gameplay on any device
  • Flexible Player Count: Support for 4-20 players with customizable spy and blank card (Mr White) options
  • Instant Setup: No registration required, start playing immediately

How to Play Undercover:

  1. Each player receives a secret word - most players get the same "civilian" word, while undercover players get a similar but different word.
  2. Players take turns describing their word without saying it directly.
  3. After each round, use our large, easy-to-tap buttons to vote on who you think is the undercover.
  4. The person with the most votes is eliminated.
  5. Game continues until either all undercover players are found (civilians win) or number of undercover equals civilians (undercover wins).

How to Play Mr White (Blank Card Mode):

  1. One or more players receive a blank card (Mr White) instead of the regular word.
  2. Mr White must listen to other players' descriptions and try to figure out the word without revealing they don't know it.
  3. Other players vote to eliminate the person they think is Mr White.
  4. If Mr White is eliminated before discovering the word, civilians win. If Mr White guesses correctly, they win!
  5. Mr White adds an extra layer of deception and challenge to the game.

Pro Tips:

  • Be Subtle: Avoid revealing whether you're a civilian, undercover, or Mr White in your descriptions!
  • Listen Carefully: Pay attention to subtle differences in how others describe their words - especially for Mr White detection
  • Mr White Strategy: If you're Mr White, listen for common themes and clues. Pay attention to what descriptions have in common!
  • Spot Mr White: Look for players whose descriptions are vague, overly general, or seem uncertain - they might be Mr White!
  • Use Categories Strategically: Different word categories offer varying difficulty levels for both Undercover and Mr White modes
  • Mobile Friendly: Our optimized interface makes voting quick and easy on phones and tablets
