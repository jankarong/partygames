How to Play Undercover Game Online

Game Features:

  • 7 Word Categories: Food & Drink, Animals, Objects, Jobs & Roles, Entertainment, Nature & Weather, and Funny
  • Custom Words: Create your own word pairs for personalized gameplay
  • Mobile Optimized: Large, touch-friendly selection buttons for easy gameplay on any device
  • Flexible Player Count: Support for 4-20 players with customizable spy and blank card options
  • Instant Setup: No registration required, start playing immediately

How to Play:

  1. Each player receives a secret word - most players get the same "civilian" word, while undercover players get a similar but different word.
  2. Players take turns describing their word without saying it directly.
  3. After each round, use our large, easy-to-tap buttons to vote on who you think is the undercover.
  4. The person with the most votes is eliminated.
  5. Game continues until either all undercover players are found (civilians win) or number of undercover equals civilians (undercover wins).

Pro Tips:

  • Be Subtle: Avoid revealing whether you're a civilian or undercover in your descriptions!
  • Listen Carefully: Pay attention to subtle differences in how others describe their words
  • Use Categories Strategically: Different word categories offer varying difficulty levels
  • Mobile Friendly: Our optimized interface makes voting quick and easy on phones and tablets

Perfect for: Office parties, family gatherings, team building events, online meetings, classroom activities, and social gatherings. The large button interface ensures everyone can participate easily!
