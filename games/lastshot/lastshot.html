🎯 How to Play Last Shot

🔧 Game Setup

  • 🔫 Host configures the number of blank chambers (1-99)
  • 💥 There is always exactly 1 bullet chamber (classic Russian Roulette)
  • 👥 Players gather around for the ultimate drinking challenge
  • 🥃 Keep drinks ready - you'll need them!

🎮 Gameplay

  1. 👤 Players take turns clicking "Pull Trigger"
  2. 🔘 Blank Chamber: You're safe! Add a shot to the pool
  3. 💥 Bullet Chamber: Bad luck! Drink ALL shots in the pool
  4. 🔄 After a bullet is fired, the round resets with a fresh revolver
  5. 🎉 Continue until you decide to stop (or can't continue!)

🎯 Strategy Tips

  • 🎲 More blank chambers = safer but longer rounds
  • 💥 Only 1 bullet chamber makes it true Russian Roulette!
  • 🔢 You can configure up to 99 blank chambers for epic games!
  • 🍺 Keep track of who's had the most shots!
  • 🎪 The tension builds as the pool grows larger
  • 📊 With N total chambers, each player has 1/N chance of getting the bullet

⚠️ Important Rules

  • 🔞 This is an adult drinking game - play responsibly
  • 🚗 Arrange safe transportation beforehand
  • 💧 Keep water available and stay hydrated
  • 🛑 Know your limits and stop if needed
