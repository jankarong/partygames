Guess This Number

📋 Game Rules

  • 🍺 Start this easy drinking games by having one person say "give me a number between 1 through 100"
  • 🎮 Perfect as 2 person drinking games or group fun - everyone takes turns to "guess this number"
  • 🔄 When "guessing the number", each player can pour any amount of drink they want
  • 🏆 The one who correctly "guess what number" it is must drink all the drinks poured by previous players
  • 🎉 Great drinking game for couples or groups - adjust drink amounts to make it more exciting!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🎮 How does this drinking game work?

One person starts by saying "give me a number between 1 through 100". Each time someone tries to "guess this number", they pour drinks. The winner who "guess what number" correctly must drink everything! Your beer mug fills up as you get closer to the correct number.

🔄 Can we play with more than 2 people?

Yes! While it's perfect as a drinking game for couples, you can play with any number of people. Each person takes turns guessing the number and pouring drinks, making it one of the most versatile easy drinking games!

📝 How much should we pour each time?

That's what makes this one of the best 2 person drinking games - you decide! When "guessing the number", each player can pour as much or as little as they want, adding strategy to the game.

🔄 What happens when someone wins?

When someone finally "guess what number" it is, they must drink all the drinks that were poured during previous guesses. It's what makes this drinking game for couples or groups so exciting!

🎯 Any strategies for playing?

If you're confident in "guess this number", pour less drink since you might win and have to drink it all! This makes it one of the most strategic easy drinking games you can play!

🍺 About Guess This Number Game

"Guess This Number" is one of the most popular easy drinking games for couples and groups. It combines the excitement of number guessing with the fun of drinking games, creating a perfect party atmosphere.

This drinking game for couples is incredibly versatile - you can play it with just two people or with a large group. The rules are simple: one person thinks of a number between 1 and 100, and others take turns guessing. With each guess, players pour drinks, and the person who guesses correctly must drink everything!

What makes this one of the best 2 person drinking games is the strategy involved. Do you pour a lot of drink when you're confident in your guess? Or do you play it safe with smaller amounts? The beer mug visualization adds an extra element of fun as it fills up based on how close your guess is.

Whether you're looking for easy drinking games for a party or a fun activity for a date night, "Guess This Number" delivers excitement and laughs. It's perfect for breaking the ice, creating memorable moments, and enjoying a fun night with friends or your partner.
