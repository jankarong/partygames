How to Create Your Own Custom Dice

  1. Click the "Customize Dice" button to open the settings.
  2. Edit existing dice or click "Add New Dice" to add more.
  3. Add your custom text or numbers in the input box and press Enter.
  4. Click the "X" next to any option to remove it.
  5. Hit the "Roll Dice!" button to see your random result!
